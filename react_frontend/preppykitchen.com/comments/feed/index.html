These were a breeze and absolutely melt-in-your-mouth delicious, as promised. I followed the directions for the recipe but cut mine a little thicker and baked seven minutes then flipped the pan around and seven more minutes. Gorgeous color and texture!

When they were fresh out of the oven and still very hot I took the sides of two straight knives and lightly tapped/squeezed the edges and ends of each cookie for a more sharper edged look. I wanted them to look more like the Walker brand shortbread biscuits and it worked like a charm.

This recipe is going in my favorites. Only double it if you can afford to eat two batches of cookies in one evening! They’re delicious!
